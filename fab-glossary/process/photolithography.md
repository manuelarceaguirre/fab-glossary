---
title: What is Photolithography?
---

Photolithography is the patterning step that transfers circuit features from a mask or reticle onto a photoresist-coated wafer.

A typical lithography loop includes coating, exposure, post-exposure bake, develop, inspection, and overlay control. Lithography links to many other fab concepts, including [photoresist](/fab-glossary/materials/photoresist), [overlay](/fab-glossary/metrology/overlay), and [critical dimension](/fab-glossary/metrology/critical-dimension).
