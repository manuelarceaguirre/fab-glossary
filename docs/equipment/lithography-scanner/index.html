What is a Lithography Scanner?

A lithography scanner is a wafer-fab exposure tool that projects the reticle pattern onto the wafer using a step-and-scan motion.

Scanners are among the most expensive tools in a leading-edge fab and are central to process-node scaling, overlay performance, and wafer throughput.
